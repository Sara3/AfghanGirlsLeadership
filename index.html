Basira Daqiq

Basira Daqiq is a first-year student at Smith College who was raised in Kabul. In 2012 she was the ground person for Afghan Women Handicraft Project, where she directed 30 women to produce handicrafts in their homes and found transportation for the products to the US to be sold. She was also a member of Afghan Women's Writing project, where participants shared real life stories to raise awareness about the violence against women in Afghanistan. A participant in Girls Leadership Worldwide and Women2women in the US in 2015, Basira founded Afghan Girls Leadership Program on the principles of those programs.
